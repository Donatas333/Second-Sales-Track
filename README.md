# Field Sales CRM MVP (Lithuania)

A lightweight, mobile‑first CRM built for Lithuanian field sales teams who manage in‑person client visits, schedule follow‑ups, and need fast, automated meeting documentation.  
This MVP focuses on simplicity, speed, and automation — helping sales reps spend less time typing and more time selling.

---

## 🌍 Business Context

Field sales teams in Lithuania (automotive parts, industrial suppliers, B2B services, etc.) often struggle with:

- Logging client visits consistently  
- Remembering follow‑ups  
- Filling in CRM fields after meetings  
- Managing tasks across many clients  
- Using overly complex CRM systems designed for managers, not reps  

As a result, important tasks get delayed, data quality drops, and sales opportunities are lost.

This MVP solves these problems with a streamlined workflow and automatic meeting documentation.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### **1. Automatic Meeting Recording & Structured Notes**
With user consent, the app records the client meeting and automatically generates structured fields such as:

- Quality of conversation  
- Client needs  
- Objections  
- Next steps  
- Follow‑up date  

This eliminates manual note‑taking and ensures consistent, high‑quality CRM data.

### **2. Visit Tracking Dashboard**
A simple, mobile‑optimized dashboard shows:

- Today’s visits  
- Overdue follow‑ups  
- Monthly activity  
- Quick actions: *New Visit* and *New Client*

### **3. Follow‑Up Management**
A dedicated follow‑up list highlights overdue tasks like:

- Sending offers  
- Delivering orders  
- Calling clients  
- Sending invoices  
- Sending catalogs  

Each task displays how many days it is overdue, helping reps prioritize their day.

### **4. Client & Visit Management**
- Add new clients  
- Log visits  
- Attach tasks  
- Track progress over time  

### **5. Lithuanian Localization**
The interface uses Lithuanian language, date formats, and terminology to match local workflows.

---

## 🧠 Why This MVP Matters

Traditional CRMs are too heavy for field reps. They require too many clicks, too much typing, and too much discipline.

This MVP is designed around **real behavior**:

- Reps want to log visits quickly  
- They forget follow‑ups unless reminded  
- They avoid long forms  
- They prefer mobile‑first tools  
- They want automation to reduce admin work  

By focusing on automation and simplicity, this tool increases:

- Data quality  
- Follow‑up reliability  
- Sales productivity  
- Manager visibility
